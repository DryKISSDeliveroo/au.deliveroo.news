Shake Up Your Mardi Gras With Deliveroo

  • 25 February 2019
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 1 min
mardigras
Share this article

Darlings, to celebrate the upcoming rainbow celebrations, Deliveroo has teamed up with LGBTQIA icon and Australia's biggest glamazon Alright, Hey to promote the Mardi Gras Shimmy & Shake with iconic burger chain Milky Lane in celebration of Mardi Gras.

The drag cream salted caramel ice-cream shake, served in a rainbow frosted glass, topped with a cream doughnut, fairy floss and finished with 100's and 1000's and lolly lips for $15 from Tuesday, February 26th to Sunday, March 2nd.

To spread the love, for every Milky Lane Mardi Gras Shimmy & Shake purchased on Deliveroo during Mardi Gras week, Deliveroo will donate $5 to Sydney Gay and Lesbian Mardi Gras registered charity.

We have a feeling Bernadette and Felicia would love this!
