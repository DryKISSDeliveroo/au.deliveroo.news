Dust off your dancing shoes, don your party hats, and throw confetti in the air as we welcome another epic year of home-delivered meals from Deliveroo. For The City of Greater Geelong, this June 28th marks two years of comfort, convenience, and countless cuisines. In other words, it denotes two years of Deliveroo's home-delivery service in Geelong - an occasion worthy of celebration.

On June 28th, frolic in the festivities as we celebrate good food and a great city. The vibrant hub of Geelong is home to a range of budding foodies - and while the locals love their chicken, the most ordered dish is Boss Fries from Boss Burger. See below some delicious data:

Most popular dishes ordered in Geelong:

  1. Tommy the Chook burger from Boss Burger Co.
  2. Boss Fries from Boss Burger Co.
  3. The Boss burger from Boss Burger
  4. Classic burrito from Zambrero
  5. Zinger Box from KFC
  6. Burger Inc fries from Burger Inc
  7. Chicken schnitzel from Schnitz
  8. Chicken tenders from The Hot Chicken Project
  9. Chicken wrap from Nando's
  10. Potato cakes from Popeyes Fish and Chippery

Impact on the Geelong community

Geelong has a distinct food culture which deserves to be celebrated. Since its launch in the city two years ago, more than 200 restaurants have signed up to the platform. Due to the demand in the delivery service, Deliveroo has created work for more than 205 Geelong locals, who have become delivery riders and members of the Deliveroo family.

Deliveroo is also supporting local businesses in Geelong, with 80% of its restaurant partners being local, independent restaurants, creating new revenue streams for homegrown culinary talent.

Levi Aron, Country Manager at Deliveroo: "Our whole team here at Deliveroo are delighted that our strong presence in Geelong over the past two years means that customers have more choice, restaurants are able to increase their revenue and reach, and riders have the opportunity to choose well-paid, flexible work. Geelong hosts a vibrant community of residents who have embraced the Deliveroo service, and we are so glad to be bringing good food to the homes, offices and people of Geelong."

About Deliveroo

Deliveroo is an award-winning delivery service founded in 2013 by William Shu and Greg Orlowski. Deliveroo works with over 80,000 best-loved restaurants and takeaways, as well as 60,000 riders to provide the best food delivery experience in the world. Deliveroo is headquartered in London, with 2,500 employees in offices around the globe.

Deliveroo operates in over 500 towns and cities across 14 markets, including Australia, Belgium, France, Germany, Hong Kong, Italy, Ireland, Netherlands, Singapore, Spain, Taiwan, United Arab Emirates, Kuwait and the United Kingdom.

Deliveroo operates in 13 cities across Australia; Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Gold Coast, Perth, Adelaide, Canberra, Geelong, Wollongong, Penrith, Newcastle, Cairns and Hobart.
